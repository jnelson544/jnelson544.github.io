A little about me....

I was born in a tiny mining town in southern Montana. I graduated high school in 2006 and was not ready for college. After bouncing around for four years and trying out several jobs, I decided to begin college for an Associates Degree in Pschology. After one year, I knew that was not the right fit for me, so I left school. Several years later, I discovered my hidden passion for computers while working as a slot machine technician outside of Missoula. It was then I decided to enroll at The University of Montana and recieve my bachelors degree in Computer Science as a Software Engineer.
